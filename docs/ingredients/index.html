Vermouth

Types There are several types of vermouth, the most common being ”dry” and “sweet”. Dry vermouth is known for its use in a martini. Brands The Martini & Rossi brand is the most readily available in the United States. Their dry vermouth is passable. But I find their sweet (called “Rosso”) to be medicinal and a quick way to ruin a drink.
