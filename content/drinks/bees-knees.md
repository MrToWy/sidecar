{{< wrap >}}
{{< ingredients
  "2 oz gin"
  "¾ oz lemon juice"
  "¾ oz honey syrup"
  "lemon twist"
>}}


{{< instructions >}}
Add gin, lemon juice, and [honey syrup](/ingredients/simple-syrups/#honey-syrup) to a cocktail shaker. Shake. Strain into coupe glass.

Garnish with lemon twist.
{{< /instructions >}}
